# JPEG, PNG & GIF
- `.jpeg`:
  - Photos
  - Too big at high quality compression
  - One layer
  - No transparency
- `.png`:
  - Digital artwork
  - Small at high quality compression
  - Allows multiple layers
  - Allows transparency
- `.gif`:
  - Only the colors it uses
  - Very small for few colors
  - Can "loop" multiple layers, like a small video
  - No transparency
